# Keyword Strategy: Skye Canyon vs Sierra at Skyeview

## Current Understanding

**Hierarchy:**
- **Skye Canyon** = Master-planned community (1,700 acres) - BROAD
  - Contains multiple builders/developments
  - More competition
  - Higher search volume
  
- **Sierra at Skyeview** = Specific development within Skye Canyon - SPECIFIC
  - Your exact development
  - Less competition
  - Lower search volume (but more qualified)

## Analysis

### "Skye Canyon" - Too Broad? ✅ YES

**Problems:**
1. **High Competition** - Multiple builders compete for "Skye Canyon homes"
2. **Less Specific** - People searching "Skye Canyon" may not want YOUR development
3. **Diluted Brand** - You're competing with other Skye Canyon developments
4. **Lower Conversion** - Broader searches = less qualified leads

**When to Use:**
- In descriptions for context ("located in Skye Canyon")
- For community amenities content
- Secondary keyword support

### "Sierra at Skyeview" - Better Focus ✅ YES

**Advantages:**
1. **Zero Competition** - No one else sells "Sierra at Skyeview" homes
2. **Highly Qualified** - People searching this know exactly what they want
3. **Brand Ownership** - You own this keyword completely
4. **Better Conversion** - Specific searches = ready-to-buy leads

**When to Use:**
- Primary keyword in titles
- H1 headings
- Main focus of content

## Recommended Strategy

### Primary Keywords (Use Most)
1. **"Sierra at Skyeview"** - Your brand, zero competition
2. **"Sierra at Skyeview Homes"** - Full brand name
3. **"Sierra at Skyeview Northwest Las Vegas"** - With location
4. **"Sierra at Skyeview 89166"** - With zip code

### Secondary Keywords (Support)
1. **"Skye Canyon"** - Use for context and broader reach
2. **"Homes in Skye Canyon"** - When describing location
3. **"Skye Canyon new construction"** - Broader market

### Long-Tail Keywords (Capture Intent)
1. **"Sierra at Skyeview homes for sale"**
2. **"Sierra at Skyeview new construction"**
3. **"Sierra at Skyeview floor plans"**
4. **"Buyer's agent Sierra at Skyeview"**
